How to plan to play the mobile game DC Legends

Leaders     Strength     Intelligence     Bleed     Healers     Taunters     Shield     Disease     Speed     Turn meter     Stamina     counter-attack    

Leaders enhance the characters, and they have special ability
Some characters are based on Strength
Some characters are based on Intelligence , they have special damage
Some characters are healers
Some characters are stamina based
Bleeders, and disease based characters attack the enby team
Some characters have counter-attack if your enby attacks
Speed and turn meter based toons give you advantage over the lower speed team
