DC Legends is a free game available on both IOS and Android platforms.

About

Dc Legends is a turn meter based mobile game released in 2016. Not to be confused with Legends of Tomorrow (which is a TV show produced by CW Network), DC Legends is very popular among teens and young adults because it includes characters based on DC Comics.

This game consists of lots of characters, and it is updated regularly. It offers in-app purchases, but you can play for free as well.

About DC Legends

Game Description

DC Legends is a Character Collecting RPG that puts players in charge of the heroes and villains of the DC Comics Multiverse in a fast-paced strategy battle game. To check out the popular characters of this game click here This game is very similar to other games such as Marvel Strike Force and Star Wars: Galaxy of Heroes in terms of gameplay.

Featuring both a Campaign Mode and PVP, the game boasts features that allow for both F2P and P2W styles of gameplay. Additionally, the game sports an Alliance System that allows players to form teams and compete against one another in a ranked ladder.
