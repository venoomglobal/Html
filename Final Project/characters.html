Dc Legends has lot of famous DC Characters from Lantern Corps, Apokolips, Krypton, Earth, and other planets.
Some Noteworthy mentions include Superman , Batman, Darksied , Lex Luthor, and others.

The characters are distinguished from each other by their affinity. There are three types of affinities in the game. Red stands for Physical, Blue for Mystical, and Green for Energy.

There are more than 100 Other characters in the game, so I will try to include the top used characters.
